FAVORITE RECIPES

BURGER

Burgers are famous for their convenience and versatility, allowing for a wide range of flavors and toppings to suit various tastes.

GOLGAPPA

Gogapa, also known as panipuri or golgappa, is famous for its unique combination of flavors and textures.

PIZZA

Pizza is famous for its delicious combination of a crispy crust, savory sauce, melted cheese, and endless topping options.

CHOLE BHATORE

Chole bhature is famous for its rich, spicy chickpea curry (chole) paired with fluffy, deep-fried bread.North Indian dish

LITTI

Litti is famous for its unique flavor and traditional preparation, featuring whole wheat balls stuffed with a spicy mix of flour.

NORTH KOREAN

North Korean cuisine is famous for its distinctive flavors and dishes, often characterized by local ingredients like kimchi..

Sushi Rolls

Sushi rolls are famous for their artful presentation and diverse flavor combinations, often featuring fresh seafood, vegetables.

Tacos al Pastor

Tacos al pastor are famous for their flavorful marinated pork, typically cooked on a vertical spit (similar to shawarma) and served.
